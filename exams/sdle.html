What the SDLE pathway actually involves

  • Eligibility first — and Saudi vs expatriate rules do not work the same way
  • DataFlow Primary Source Verification comes before Mumaris Plus classification for foreign documents
  • Mumaris Plus generates the 1-year eligibility number that unlocks Prometric booking
  • The exam is now built around the modern 200-question structure and a scaled passing score of 542
  • FDI tooth numbering, infection control, ethics, and Saudi legal logic are built into the clinical reasoning
  • Passing the SDLE gives you Classification — not the final legal license to practise
