What the ADC pathway actually involves

  • Initial assessment first — this is the entry gate to the exam pathway
  • Written examination second — 280 scenario-based questions over two days
  • Practical examination third — three cases, eight tasks, held in Melbourne
  • English language rules and AHPRA registration sit after ADC, not inside the written or practical exam itself
  • Written venue booking runs through Pearson VUE and both days must be booked at the same centre
  • Written exam validity is now 5 years for passes from March 2026 onward
