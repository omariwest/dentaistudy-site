What the UAE dental path actually is

  • There is no single nationwide UAE dental licence — DHA, MOH, and DOH are separate authorities with separate portals and exam flows
  • DHA and MOH use Prometric, while DOH uses Pearson VUE
  • DHA is the most time-pressured route in 2026 because of the 170 minute format change
  • The three-attempt rule is shared across all three authorities, not three attempts per authority
  • DataFlow PSV is the universal gate before the system really moves
  • Passing the exam does not activate your licence by itself — final registration still depends on employer or facility activation
  • EDREE is separate from licensing exams and belongs to the UAE residency path, not the general dentist work route
