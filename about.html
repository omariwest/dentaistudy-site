About

Why DentAIstudy exists

Built by people who actually went through dental school, OSCE checklists, and viva anxiety.

Our mission

DentAIstudy was created with one clear goal: to make studying dentistry simpler, clearer, and more structured. We help students transform dense topics, scattered notes, and overwhelming textbooks into organized, exam-focused study flows.

Who we are

We are a small team of dental residents, educators, and AI‑driven learners who deeply understand academic pressure, OSCE preparation, case presentations, and the complexity of dental subjects. Our tools are built from real-world studying experiences—not theory.

  • We understand how demanding dental exams truly are
  • We write and use case notes, OSCE checklists, and structured flows daily
  • We’re driven by making dental education clearer and more efficient

What DentAIstudy helps you do

  • Convert heavy chapters into OSCE-style checklists and organized summaries
  • Simplify classifications, definitions, steps, and clinical reasoning
  • Generate clean, ready-to-use prompts you can copy or refine
  • Stay focused with a distraction‑free, minimal interface

What DentAIstudy is not

DentAIstudy is a study assistant, not a clinical authority. It does not replace official textbooks, curriculum guidelines, or supervision from qualified educators. Always verify generated content against trusted academic sources and your institution’s references.

What we care about

These values guide how we design every page and every feature.

Exam realism

We aim to mirror how examiners think: history, exam, investigations, management, classifications and viva points.

Cognitive simplicity

Less scrolling, less noise. Just the structure you need when your brain is tired before a clinic or exam.

Clarity over hype

We design for precise, honest study support—not marketing buzzwords or overcomplicated dashboards.
